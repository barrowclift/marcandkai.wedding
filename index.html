Marc & Khary

May 21, 2022
Philadelphia

It all started with casual conversations over coffee, but not the way you’d expect.

Back in 2017, Khary Bethea worked as a cafe barista in the Comcast Center, and the new Comcast hire, Marc Barrowclift, swung by regularly for a cup. Those frequent visits weren’t for the coffee, but rather the friendly talks they shared in the process, which broadly ranged from their favorite games to that weekend’s plans. After many similar visits, Marc finally drummed up the courage to pass Khary a Post-it with his number, ask if he’d like to “hang out” sometime, and calmly walked frantically scurried away before Khary could respond.

Thankfully, just a few minutes later, he did.

From there on, they flew. There were many a family barbecue, hundreds of board & card games, and eventually eager apartment searching together. With support from each other, they also grew independently in their careers, with Khary’s many years of hard work paying off with promotion & career change to Pantry Manager and Marc rising to the challenge of helping lead critical team initiatives.

After living together for a couple years and still enjoying every minute of it, they visited a local jeweler to learn what each other’s ring sizes are and what kinds of styles they gravitated towards. The verbal intention was for each other to then know these details for a future purchase together. Unbeknownst to Kai, Marc returned the very next day to place the order.

Just a brief time later at that summer’s yearly Pine Point family vacation, Khary Bethea and Marc Barrowclift were engaged.
